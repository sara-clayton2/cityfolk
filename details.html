HOURS

Gates open at 5 PM on weekdays, 2 PM on weekends. Headliners end at about 10 PM nightly, and late night programming (Aberdeen/Marvest) at about 1 AM.

BOXOFFICE

Tickets will be sold at the CityFolk box office, located adjacent to the TD Place box office on Exhibition Way. This opens at 4 PM on event weekdays, 1PM on event weekends.
Note that pricing is subject to change, and we cannot predict wait times on busy evenings. Purchase your tickets in advance for the smoothest festival experience.

RE-ENTRY

Patrons can leave and re-enter the festival. You MUST scan out BEFORE leaving the park if you intend to return later that day – no exceptions!
Please see a gates attendant PRIOR to exiting the festival gates if you are unclear on how this works.

SEATING

The Great Lawn is a general admission (GA) event NOT held at TD Place (the stadium) but behind it.
There is no seating in GA. Patrons may bring collapsible lawn chairs to use in the designated lawnchair section located on the North side of the hill only. If you are bringing a lawn chair:

Share your space. Fold chairs when not in use.

Please don't block paths or entrances.

Note - there are no lawncharis allowed at the RavenLaw Stage due to space limitations.

CHILDREN

Children under 10 do not need a ticket when accompanied by a parent.
We recommend bringing earplugs and planning a meeting spot in advance with young children in case of separation. Not all acts are suitable for all audiences.
BSOMA Kidzone activities are planned on weekend afternoons from 1-6PM. These activities are free! See more on the Participate page.

STUDENT RUSH" SAME-DAY TICKET

School may be back in session, but the fun isn’t over just yet! Student rush passes will be available each day at CityFolk. Discounted student passes will be available at the festival box office daily. Arrive early to get a deal!

HERE'S THE SCOOP

Availability – A limited number of “Student Rush” same day tickets will be available each day starting when the box office opens (weeknights: 4 PM, weekend: 2 PM). Students can purchase up to two (2) tickets each day at the discounted price.

PRICING

Student rush tickets are $30 each day, EXCEPT on Sept. 18 ($40). Eligibility – Purchasers must be a current student. Please bring or show proof of enrollment. This can be a student card, timetable, tuition receipt, etc.

ACCESSIBILTY

The festival is an accessible event. Paid underground handicap parking and Para-Transpo drop-off is available at Lansdowne. Accessibility drop-off location is along Exhibition Way, near the South-West corner of Aberdeen Pavilion. The terrain is largely flat and there are paved pathways.
Patrons with a disability who require an attendant can bring one attendant to the festival at no extra cost. Please see a gates attendant when you arrive.
VIP Lounge reserved seating is not wheelchair friendly. Please let us know if you are looking to purchase reserved seating with wheelchair access. There i s a separate accessibility platform at the TELUS stage for patrons in a wheelchair and one attendant to use if needed.
RavenLaw Stage is accessible, however, there is no reserved accessibility seating available at this stage.

EXCHANGES/REFUNDS

Tickets are sold to the festival date and not necessarily refunded or exchanged due to variations in lineup.

LOST AND FOUND

During the festival, items will be held at the Festival Administration tent (located between Aberdeen Pavilion and the main gate). Please stop by to claim your item.

HOURS ARE

10:00 AM – 10:15 PM Daily
Post-event we retain items for one month for pickup at our festival office. All items of value are then taken to Ottawa Police Services.
